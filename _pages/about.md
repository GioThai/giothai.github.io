I am a graduate applied mathematics student at California Polytechnic State University in San Luis Obispo, CA. My research interests lie in mathematically modeling biological and social systems. My master's thesis at Cal Poly involves numerically simulating the dynamics between chronic myelogenous leukemia (CML), continuous medication, and immune response. I see the value in understanding methods in mathematical biology as a stepping stone to analyzing complex social systems. My previous research incorporates other intriguing fields like graph theory, game theory, and combinatorics.

Feel free to check out a snapshot of previous [research projects](https://giothai.github.io/publications/), as well as my [academic profile](https://giothai.github.io/cv/).
