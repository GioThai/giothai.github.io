Chronic Myelogenous Leukemia (CML) results from the uncontrolled growth of white blood cells in the bone marrow. We model a system of delay-differential equations monitoring cancer cell dynamics under continuous treatment (imatinib) and immune response. We implement variations in model parameters and strategically timed treatment interruptions to investigate patient recovery outcomes.
